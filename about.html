About Us

The Russo family tradition of hospitality started over 50 years ago when the Villa Russo in Richmond Hill opened its doors. Frank Russo Sr. believed that exemplary service and exquisite cuisine combined with professional attention to detail was the best way to achieve customer satisfaction. Those beliefs were passed down to his son, Frank Russo Jr. who opened Russo’s on the bay in 1987. We have had the honor of hosting events spanning some of our customers lifetimes from baptisms to communions to sweet 16’s to weddings. Our senior management team has over 200 years combined experience directing the most lavish events and parties. The most memorable of occasions call for an exemplary standard in catering.
